## Description

Noted States is a private AI field journal for curious travelers.

Read a place before you arrive, ask about what you are seeing, and capture the thought before it disappears. Noted States organizes your private notes by place and meaning, then helps you return to the questions and patterns that shaped a journey.

Use Noted States to:

- ask place-specific questions using public reference sources
- dictate or type private field notes
- save observations, conversations, food signals, farmstay moments, and reflections
- view your own memories on a map
- optionally recognize a new state or county while Journey Mode is active
- turn private observations into a route-free Journey Portrait share card
- search and revisit an on-device Library
- share selected notes through the iOS share sheet

Noted States is not an audio tour, route tracker, trip planner, or public review network. It helps you understand what you saw and remember what you noticed.

Private by default. Nothing is published from Noted States.
