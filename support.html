Support

Waymark Help

Ask is unavailable

Ask needs an internet connection. Check the place name, add the state, and try again. Capture and Library remain available without Ask.

Microphone is unavailable

Open iOS Settings, find Waymark U.S., and review Microphone and Speech Recognition access. You can always type a note instead.

Delete my data

On the web, choose “Delete local data” in the footer. In the iOS app, delete individual Library records or use Delete All Local Notes in Privacy.

Important limits

Waymark does not publish notes and is not emergency, safety, medical, legal, navigation, or official travel guidance. Verify important information using primary sources.

Contact

The monitored support contact is provided in the App Store listing for the current release.
